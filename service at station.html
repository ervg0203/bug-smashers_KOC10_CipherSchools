SERVICES AT STATION

FOOD AND DRINKS


Feed your empty tummy with the healthy
meal. Look out for various food vendors and
stalls at each platform which serves the best
quality food. Let your journey begin with
healthy and tasteful meal.

WAITING AREAS

Do not let yourself stand for prolonged
hours on station. Make your body and
soul relax at the executive restrooms. Avail this
first class facility at the entrance of every station.
Restrooms are facilitated with air conditioners
ample of charging points and proper hygiene.

WIFI FACILITY


Begin your journey with entertainment. Railway
stations are now equipped with new wifi facilities
which serves you the best speed and make your
train journey less boring. Scroll down the
menu bar in your device and click on the RAILWAYS.
Enter your PNR number in password and access
the world class internet.
